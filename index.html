About Me

Howdy! My name is Nicholas Martinez and I'm passionate about accessibility. I'm taking this class because I have several friends and family members with disabilities. I think it's important that everyone should have equal access to both information and technology; I wanted to be a part of making the web a more accessible place so that my friends and others like them can have a more positive experience online.
